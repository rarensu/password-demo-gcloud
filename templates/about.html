How does it work?

In addition to storing one password hash on the server, I store the password hash for each deletion typo of the password.
When checking a password guess, I also check each deletion typo of the guess.
For most simple typos, such as substitution or tranposition, there will be a match.

Is it safe?

In theory, typo tolerant passwords are safe. Here's a paper about it.
https://dl.acm.org/doi/10.1145/3133956.3134000
